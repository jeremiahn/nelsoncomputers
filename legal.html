Privacy Policy

At Nelson Computers, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to perform repairs and communicate with you, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Device passwords (stored securely and only used for repair purposes).
  • Device serial numbers and technical specifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the purpose of diagnosing, repairing, and returning your device. We do not sell your data to third parties. We may use your email to send invoice receipts or repair status updates.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data while your device is in our possession. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

Terms of Service

By submitting your device to Nelson Computers for repair, you agree to the following terms.

1. Authorization

You authorize Nelson Computers to perform diagnostics and repairs on your device. You understand that repair attempts can sometimes result in data loss or further hardware failure, especially with liquid-damaged devices.

2. Data Backup

It is your responsibility to back up your data prior to repair. Nelson Computers is not responsible for any data loss that may occur during the repair process.

3. Limited Warranty

We provide a 90-day warranty on all labor and parts replaced by us. This warranty does not cover:

  • New physical damage (cracked screens, dents).
  • Liquid damage incurred after the repair.
  • Software issues unrelated to the hardware repair.

4. Unclaimed Devices

Devices left for over 60 days after notification of repair completion (or unfixable status) may be considered abandoned and recycled to recoup costs.
